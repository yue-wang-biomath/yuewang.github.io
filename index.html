About Me

I'm Yue, a Ph.D candidate working at the Dr. Jia Meng's lab [google scholar], at the Faculty of Science and Engineering, University of Liverpool.

My PhD project is to make statistical and deep learning approaches for characterizing RNA methylation with respect to functional components and gene structures. I have knowledge in bioinformatics, biostatistics, probability, machine learning and deep learning. I have a broad interest in lots of bioinformatics questions and seek to conduct sound and interesting science. Please check out this website for more information on my skills and research projects.
